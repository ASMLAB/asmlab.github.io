The tight coupling between the atmosphere and the ocean is continuously shaping our climate by exchanging momentum, heat, freshwater and gases such as carbon dioxide.
The Air-Sea Modeling Laboratory strives to better understand the air-sea coupling and its role on our climate in both global and regional scales.
